# AI-Scheduling-App
A simple command-line tool that helps you plan your weekly schedule and achieve your goals using AI-powered coaching from Claude.
Overview
AI Life Planner allows you to input your weekly schedule with time blocks for each activity and set personal goals. The app uses Anthropic's Claude AI to analyze your schedule and provide personalized, actionable tips to help you:

Identify time conflicts and overlapping activities
Improve work-life balance
Find time for goal-related activities
Optimize your time management
Stay on track with your objectives

Features

Simple Command-Line Interface: No complex GUI - just straightforward prompts
Flexible Scheduling: Enter activities with time blocks in any format
Overlap Detection: Activities can overlap, and the AI will identify conflicts
Goal Tracking: Set short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals
AI-Powered Analysis: Get 5-7 personalized tips based on your unique schedule
Categorized Feedback: Tips organized by time management, habits, priorities, balance, and strategy
