# AI-Scheduling-App
A simple command line app that helps you plan your weekly schedule and achieve your goals using Claude's LLM.
Overview
This app allows you to input your weekly schedule with time blocks for each activity and set personal goals. 
The app uses Anthropic's Claude AI to analyze your schedule and provide personalized, actionable tips.

Helps you:
- Identify time conflicts and overlapping activities
- Improve work-life balance
- Find time for goal-related activities
- Optimize your time management
- Stay on track with your objectives

Features:
- Flexible Scheduling: Enter activities with time blocks in any format
- Overlap Detection: Activities can overlap, and the AI will identify conflicts
- Goal Tracking: Set short term, medium term, and long term goals
- AI-Powered Analysis: Get  personalized tips based on your unique schedule
- Categorized Feedback: Tips organized by time management, habits, priorities, balance, and strategy
